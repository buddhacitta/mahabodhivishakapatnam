About Us

Mahabodhi Visakhapatnam

A branch of the renowned Mahabodhi Society Bengaluru, dedicated to spreading the timeless wisdom of Buddha and fostering spiritual growth in the vibrant city of Visakhapatnam.

Our Heritage

The Mahabodhi Society was founded in 1891 by the venerable Anagarika Dharmapala in Colombo, Sri Lanka. Our Bengaluru branch has been serving the spiritual needs of seekers for decades, and now we extend this sacred mission to Visakhapatnam, bringing authentic Buddhist teachings to the coastal region of Andhra Pradesh.
